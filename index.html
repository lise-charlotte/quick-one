Squad Busters list

Characters and are they good? Subjective and current list

  1. Archer - she's cool. Her power is pretty okay, but better in Lava World than other worlds.
  2. Archer Queen - yes. She does so much. Powers up the attack speed of the team and heals herself. At 4 stars drops an X-Bow spell. Useful. 10/10.
  3. Bandit - gives 15 gems upon choosing her, also drops gems when killing certain monsters (most, but not the small ones). Dies fast though if left unfused.
  4. Barbarian - yeah he's cool. Seeing two SB youtubers choosing him quite a bit, not completely in on the hype though.
  5. Barbarian King - absolutely yes. Drops a Super Rage spell at 4 stars. OP. Overall great.
  6. Battle Healer - I liker her. She makes other characters have better HP. Also heals upon opening chests.
  7. Bea - low HP, dies fast if left unfused. Fuse and get those bees on the map.
